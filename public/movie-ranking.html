The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gladiator

General gets framed for killing the emperor who was going to name him second in command.

Goes on a revenge tour by becoming a gladiator, beating everyone all the way to the top.

300

The story of 300 Spartans who held back the Persian army at the Hot Gates.

Brutal decapitations and shield-bashing galore.

Interstellar

Mind-bending time travel, strange planets, and a black hole make up this one-of-a-kind space romp.
